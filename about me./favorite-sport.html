Favorite Sport

I began playing basketball in 5th grade and have loved it ever since. I try to go play pick up games or shoot around with my friends whenever I have free time. I find it to be both a fun time and good exercise. Additionally, I enjoy keeping up-to-date with what is happening inside of the National Basketball Association.

One of my favorite basketball moments is this picture that I took a few years ago of Kevin Durant. I am from California so I was able to go to a Golden State Warriors game and saw some of my favorite players including Stephen Curry, Klay Thompson, and Kevin Durant.
