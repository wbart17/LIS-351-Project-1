Favorite Food

Being half-Japanese, I have been exposed to Japanese cuisine since I was born. Although I like much of which the cuisine has to offer, sushi has definitely become my favorite. My favorite type of sushi is nigiri, which is a slice of raw fish over rice.

The picture attached is one that I took the last time that I visited Tokyo, Japan. There are several different types of tuna on the board, my favorite being the toro.
